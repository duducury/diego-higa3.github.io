About Higa

Diego Higa, a professional drift racer from Brazil, has made a significant impact in the drifting world since starting at the age of 14. With numerous championships under his belt, including being an 8-time Super Drift Brasil Champion and the Netflix Hyperdriver Champion, Diego's journey is nothing short of inspirational.
